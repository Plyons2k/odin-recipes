french toast

Description

The recipe today is on french toast. The ingredients are found below.

The recipe will consist of the usual eggs bread and milk with a special twist for added flavor

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • eggs
  • milk
  • cinnamon
  • whip cream
  • vanilla extract

Steps

  1. take eggs and mix them in a bowl
  2. add a little bit of cinnamon in the bowl
  3. make a 4th of the bowl with milk(1/4th milk 3/4ths eggs)
  4. add whipcream to the mix and other addons and mix
  5. allow the bread to lightly touch the mixture in the flat bowl on both sides
  6. Place on skillet and allow to get golden brown before eating
  7. enjoy
